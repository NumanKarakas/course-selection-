Strategies for Success from fellow Matadors

Student 1: This is a nicely paced class, and it focuses on making sure each and every topic is covered in a way which ensures conceptual understanding. Take notes in class if you are finding the topics confusing. Tests are rather frequent, but focusing in class, and completing the few homework assignments should prepare you well. Pay attention in class, and complete assignments, and there shouldn't be too much outside work.
