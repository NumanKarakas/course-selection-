Myth and Folklore Course Description

Strategies for Success from fellow Matadors

Student 1: This course is pretty similar to the World Literature course, but is more focused on Mythology and folklore, and tales etc. Most of the work is completed during class, so make sure to ask the teacher, or other classmates if you are having trouble understanding the deeper meaning of the material.

Course Description: This course is an introduction to mythology and folklore of a country or group of people. Students analyze the relationship between myths and various cultures in order to highlight universal themes in mythology, discuss the significance of literary devices from classical and contemporary literature, and build vocabulary from words in the literature. Extensive expository and analytical activities, writing and speaking experiences are integrated.
