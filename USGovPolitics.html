US Government and Politics Course Description

Strategies for Success from fellow Matadors

Student 1: Pay attention during class, and take notes. Make sure to do "active studying" instead of just reading the textbook, or the notes. Watch videos online(Heimlers history), to help understand the material, and take notes during the videos to help you understand the topics that will be on the test. There is a fair amount of discussions and focus around current political topics.

Course Decription: The goals of this course are to give an understanding of democratic processes and an awareness of the values and social framework that support them. Major units in the course are federal government, state government, political parties, elections, and selected issues of government in the United States.
