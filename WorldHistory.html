Strategies for Success from fellow Matadors

Student 1: This is a very fun class, and really makes the students think about the history of our soceity. Make sure to work diligently during class, and complete all the work in the class period. If all the work is completed in class, there is almost no homework from this class.

Course Description: In this course, students examine major turning points in the shaping of the modern world and the rise of Democratic ideas from the late eighteenth century to the present. The year begins with an introduction to current world issues and then continues with a focus on the expansion of the West and the growing interdependence of people and cultures throughout the world including Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.
